ITQM 2019 is a global forum for exchanging research findings and case studies that bridge the latest information technology and quantitative management techniques. It explores how the use of information technology improves quantitative management techniques and how the development of management tools can reshape the development of information technology. It particularly addresses the advancement of information technology and its impacts on global business competition in Big Data era.

ITQM 2019 covers all topics in the broad ranges of Information Technology and quantitative management, including, but not limited to:
