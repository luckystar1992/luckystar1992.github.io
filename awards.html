1. IAITQM Awards：
Richard Price Award in Data Science
The award is in honor of Richard Price in recognition of his pioneer contribution to the field of data science. It is presented by the International Academy of Information Technology and Quantitative Management to a person who made distinguished contributions to the theory, methodology, and applications of data science.
Walter Scott Award in Information Technology
The award is in honor of Walter Scott, Jr. in recognition of his life-time dedication to information technology education and applications. It is presented by the International Academy of Information Technology and Quantitative Management to a person who established a remarkable record of creativity in enhancing the theory, algorithm, education and applications of information technology.
Siwei Cheng Award in Quantitative Management
The award is in honor of Siwei Cheng in recognition of his fundamental and innovative contribution to quantitative management. It is presented by the International Academy of Information Technology and Quantitative Management to a person who devoted genius effort to applying quantitative methods and information technology to solve management problems.
Daniel Berg Award in Technology and Service Systems
The award is in honor of Daniel Berg in recognition of his distinguished achievement in technology and service sector. It is presented by the International Academy of Information Technology and Quantitative Management to a person who made important contribution to services system, technology innovation, and strategic decision making.
2. IJITDM：
Herbert Simon Award
Herbert Simon Award is for Outstanding Contribution in International Journal of Information Technology and Decision Making (IJITDM). Herbert Alexander Simon (June 15, 1916 – February 9, 2001) was a great thinker and scholar. He received many honors, including the ACM's Turing Award (1975) and the Nobel Memorial Prize in Economics (1978). His works in artificial intelligence, information processing, decision-making, problem-solving, and complex systems have fundamentally been influencing research activities in Information Technology and Decision Making. World Scientific Publishing Co., Inc. sponsors the establishment of Herbert Simon Award for Outstanding Contribution in Information Technology and Decision Making since 2011.
